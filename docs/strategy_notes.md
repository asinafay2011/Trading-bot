# Strategy Notes

Use this file to document insights, backtesting results, and adjustments to your trading strategy.  Keeping detailed notes will help you refine your bot over time and provide context for changes.

- **Backtesting**: Describe your backtesting methodology and performance metrics.
- **Parameter tuning**: Document the results of adjusting moving average lengths, RSI thresholds, and stop‑loss distances.
- **Observations**: Record notable market conditions (e.g., high volatility, news events) and how they affected your strategy.
